PurdueEats

PurdueEats is an Android app for students at Purdue University to explore off-campus dining options. I created this app with my team at Hello World 2018, Purdue's freshman-only hackathon. This project was built in Android Studio, and was written in Java.


Problem Motivation

Purdue University has several mobile apps for on-campus dining options, such as dining courts and cafes. However, during my freshman fall semester, I struggled finding off-campus dining options that suited my tastes. I wanted to create an app for students to find student-recommended restaurants and businesses so that they can broaden their dining options, as well as support local businesses.

App Features

  • Search restaurants nearby Purdue's campus
  • Provide information about dining options, including hours, menus, and location.
  • Links to restaurant websites for users to get more details.
